Mac and Cheese
Information about Mac and Cheese

    Mac and Cheese is a dish of cooked macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce, usually cheddar.
    Growing up, this has been one of my favorite meals because I love pasta and this has always been one of the most convenient ways to make it.
    I also love the cheesy taste of it.

Recipe for Boxed Mac and Cheese

  1. Boil 6 cups of water in a pot until it begins to bubble rapidly
  2. Place macaroni in the pot and wait about 8 minutes
  3. Drain the water from the macaroni
  4. Place butter, milk, and the cheese sauce mix into the pot
  5. Stir the ingredients together
  6. Enjoy!
